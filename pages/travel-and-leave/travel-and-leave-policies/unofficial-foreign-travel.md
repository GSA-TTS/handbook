## Reporting Personal Foreign Travel

### Before You Go 

1. Complete the [Foreign Travel Notification Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdlipxr1EjBcKarngQv5f4Djfi-d31ZUD8ZrobtWPQQkuML9w/viewform). If you have issues with the form, please email [threat-management-office@gsa.gov](threat-management-office@gsa.gov). 
2. Learn about your destination at the [State Department’s Travel Advisory](https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/travel-advisories.html) page. 
3. Review [training material and foreign travel training slides](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1hjBXG1jIlizaTTNjLjV7_1wernMtCfMK/view)

**\*Note:** If the location you are planning to travel to is currently listed as a Travel Advisory Level 4, you **must** contact [threat-management-office@gsa.gov](threat-management-office@gsa.gov) to schedule a one-on-one travel briefing.

### When You Return 

1. Within five days of your return from foreign travel, complete the [Foreign Travel De-briefing form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeTeqBBLYs3zGLBKepxpmu7vqhSDxNUYo7n4tEk3A6vDn9yXQ/viewform). If you have issues with the form, please email [threat-management-office@gsa.gov](threat-management-office@gsa.gov).

### For More Information

This information is derived from the GSA Insite page, [Report your foreign travel](https://insite.gsa.gov/employee-resources/security-and-emergency-management/report-your-foreign-travel?term=foreign). Please refer to that page for more information and the most up-to-date resources.
